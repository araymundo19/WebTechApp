Cebu: History, Adventure, and Island Life

The "Queen City of the South" seamlessly blends rich colonial history with modern urban living and breathtaking natural wonders.

Historical Landmarks in Cebu City

Step back in time by visiting key historical sites like Magellan's Cross, a significant symbol of the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines. Explore Basilica Minore del Santo Niño, the oldest Roman Catholic church in the country, and Fort San Pedro, a historical military defense structure.

Southern Cebu: Waterfalls and Whalesharks

Venture south to discover stunning natural attractions. Kawasan Falls offers a multi-tiered cascade of turquoise waters, perfect for canyoneering. In Oslob, you can have the unique experience of swimming alongside gentle whale sharks (though responsible tourism practices are encouraged).

Things to Do:

  • Visit Magellan's Cross and Basilica del Santo Niño
  • Explore Fort San Pedro
  • Canyoneering at Kawasan Falls
  • Swim with whalesharks in Oslob (research ethical operators)
  • Island hopping to nearby Mactan resorts
