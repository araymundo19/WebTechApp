Discover the Wonders of Palawan

Known as the "Last Frontier" of the Philippines, Palawan offers breathtaking lagoons, pristine beaches, and diverse marine life.

El Nido & Coron: Island Hopping Paradise

The northern parts of Palawan, particularly El Nido and Coron, are famed for their dramatic limestone karsts, secret lagoons, and white-sand beaches. Island hopping tours here offer unparalleled beauty, allowing visitors to swim in hidden coves and snorkel in crystal-clear waters.

Puerto Princesa: Underground River Adventure

The capital city, Puerto Princesa, is home to the stunning UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park. Take a paddle boat ride through this incredible cave system, marveling at its unique rock formations and diverse ecosystem.

Things to Do:

  • Island hopping tours (El Nido & Coron)
  • Visit the Puerto Princesa Underground River
  • Scuba diving and snorkeling
  • Explore Honda Bay
  • Relax on Nacpan Beach
