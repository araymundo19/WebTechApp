Thailand: Land of Smiles and Wonders

Discover vibrant street life, majestic temples, and stunning tropical islands in a country known for its incredible hospitality.

Bangkok: A City of Contrasts

Start your Thai adventure in Bangkok, a bustling metropolis where ancient traditions meet modern skyscrapers. Explore the Grand Palace, Wat Arun, and Wat Pho (home of the Reclining Buddha). Indulge in world-famous street food and vibrant night markets.

Southern Islands: Tropical Paradise

Head south to discover Thailand's famous islands like Phuket, Krabi, Koh Phi Phi, and Koh Samui. Relax on pristine beaches, go snorkeling or diving in turquoise waters, and enjoy the lively beach parties or tranquil resorts.

Things to Do:

  • Visit the Grand Palace and temples in Bangkok
  • Explore floating markets
  • Enjoy Thai street food and cooking classes
  • Island hopping in Phuket, Krabi, or Koh Phi Phi
  • Experience a traditional Thai massage
